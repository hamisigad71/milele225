MOMBASA BRANCH

Mombasa is a coastal city located in southeastern Kenya, along the shores of the Indian Ocean. It is the country's second-largest city and serves as the capital of Mombasa County.
History: Mombasa has a rich history that dates back centuries. It has been a significant trading port for various cultures, including the Arabs, Portuguese, and British. This history is reflected in the city's architecture, cuisine, and culture.
Mombasa is not only a significant economic center but also a Educational supporting city, cultural and historical treasure of Kenya. Its coastal location, rich history, and vibrant culture make it a fascinating place to visit for tourists and an essential part of Kenya's identity.
